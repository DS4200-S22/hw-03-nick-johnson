HTML+CSS & Marks+Channels

The data in this case are sequential quantitative attributes which is depicted through the bar heights as well as the bucket sizes for each. The Marks for this visual encoding is area of the bars, and the channels are colors, vertical and horizontal position, and areas of the sections in each bar. The marks are used as items/nodes.

The data in this case are sequential attributes which is shown through the years and the lines through each of the rows of letters. Marks for this visual encoding are the points and the lines are connection links while the channels are color and horizontal and vertical position.

The data in this case are categorical data where each of the ideas connected to the main one would be subtopics. The Marks for this visual encoding are connection links connecting the attributes and subtopics through the lines, while the channels are area, lines, and color.

The data in this case are examples of 3 sections of quantitative sequential data. The Marks for this visual encoding is areas (each pie chart), and tilt is channel. The marks would be items/nodes in this scenario. The limits are through the x and y axis.
